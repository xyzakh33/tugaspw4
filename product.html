ICYMI

ICYMI (In Case You Missed It) is EDEN’s third full-length album. In December of 2021, EDEN performed seven unreleased songs as part of his End Credits Anniversary Tour. Four of the songs made it onto this album. The four songs are “Modern Warfare”, “Balling”, “Sci-Fi” and “Call Me Back”.

No Future

no future is Irish singer-songwriter and producer, EDEN’s sophomore album with 19 songs and a running length of 57 minutes. The album was released on February 14th, 2020.

I Think You Think Too Much Of Me

EDEN's latest release is available on 12" Vinyl and Compact Cassette, a deliberate choice made by Ng who dislikes CDs. The B-sides are remasters of earlier works, including XO, Fumes, and Circles. Fumes features an added verse by Gnash. Music videos were created for "sex" and "drugs", with "drugs" also receiving a VR experience video.

Vertigo

EDEN discussed his album "vertigo" with Atwood Magazine, stating that the album itself is the best description of its meaning. The album was heavily teased on social media, with a scavenger hunt for the second single "gold" in 16 cities worldwide. The album was released on January 19th, 2018, with three singles preceding it: "start//end", "gold", and "crash". To celebrate the release, EDEN participated in live-streams, a Reddit AMA, and a live Q&A with Spotify, giving fans a glimpse into his personal side and the album's creation.
